In the Beginning

Rent A Pet began with a family that loved dogs. George Smith and his wife Carol owned two Irish Setters. These dogs were pets to the Smith family children and extended family members.

One day as George was walking his dogs in the park, he noticed how they attracted the attention of passing women. A young woman remarked that she would love to have a big dog like that to walk, but she had a small apartment and could not keep a large dog.

This encounter inspired an idea. What if there were others who wanted companionship from pets without the responsibility of ownership? George shared his idea with Carol, and they began exploring the possibility of starting a pet rental business.

Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages

  • Renters could have access to pets at their leisure
  • Renters could choose the pet they wanted
  • Renters could exercise with pets
  • Renters could enjoy companionship
  • Renters would not pay upkeep or medical expenses
  • Owners could enjoy many pets

Disadvantages

  • The business must provide housing and feeding
  • The business must cover medical expenses
  • The business must carry liability insurance
  • The owners must provide constant care

What Makes Us Unique

Rent A Pet has expanded from its early location in Provo and now has franchises in Logan, Bountiful, Salt Lake City, and St. George.

The company employs trained veterinarians and animal trainers who work with the dogs and horses daily. Rent A Pet has attracted many satisfied customers, including senior citizens, young adults, and event planners.

Pets are rented for companionship, exercise, parties, movies, commercials, and special occasions such as Halloween.
