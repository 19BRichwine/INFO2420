What was your first impression of this site?: This website seems to be well-made at first glance. The colors and links are very cohesive and user friendly.

How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?): Most of the links are on the left side, to navigate to different places like products and recipes. There is a search function and more informational links on the top right. The main menu is in the top left as well, though.

Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?: It is easy to navigate, if I want to shop it is pretty obvious where to go. If i need recipes (using their ingredients) there is a place for that. If I need to find my local store, it's easy to change.

Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?: There is a lot, and I mean a lot, of things going on in this main page. Lots of pop-ups immediately for mailing list signups, cookies, details, and other announcements. It can be a little overwhelming.

How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?: It loaded very quickly and when navigating around the load-times are also very quick and easy.

Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?: The text color matches their theme very well, and the size seems normal and easier to read than expected. There is a lot of background color, but it enhances more than anything since it doesn't clash.

Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?: Very easy to navigate if you're familiar with the layout of modern websites. If you're unaware of the 3 line menu button, it wouldn't be very easy to get around.

Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?: The color scheme was very on brand for their company, and it allows for confidence that you are on the right sight even as you navigate.

How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?: It resizes well, and quickly. Making it take half of the screen doesn't seem to make the website harder to use.

What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?: This site offers a simple navigation pane that has pictures that can draw your eyes to the places you need. If you need food, there's a picture of a banana among other pictures that have to do with the different categories.
